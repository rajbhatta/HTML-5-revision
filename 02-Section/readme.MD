## What is section tag? ##
A section can be used to group things together thematically. There are two ways to use section tags
- section tag inside article tag
- section tag outside article tag

## Section tag inside the article tag ##
```html

        <h2> Big Products</h2>
        <article>
            <h3>Products 1</h3>
```
